Dr. Vishal Parekh is affiliated with Cornell University, NY. After attaining fellowship in nephrology at Emory University, GA and work experience in Athens, GA in nephrology, he has now established his practice in nephrology in Cumming, GA. Dr. Parekh has more than 15 years experience in Medicine, and since 2007, he has provided care to numerous individuals in several communities through 14 hospitals within 6 hospitals systems in GA.
Dr. Ebie Varghese is a Board-certified Nephrologist who has been in practice for more than 20 years. After graduating from Calicut Medical College, India in 1992, Dr. Varghese completed internship and residency from New York Medical College, and fellowship in Nephrology from University of New Mexico in Albuquerque, NM. He recently relocated from Arizona where he has been practicing for the last 12 years.
